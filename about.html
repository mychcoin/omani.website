About Omani.Website

Omani.Website is owned by MYCH Business Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

MYCH stands for "Make Your Customers Happy". We are a start-up company established with a vision of developing products and delivering services that can benefit the mankind beyond limited horizons.

We draw inspiration from our belief that everyone can contribute in finding meaningful needs of the present environment and can work towards finding a solution in search of a better future.

MYCH is an ISO 9001:2008 and ISO 27001:2013 Certified Company

Focus on Oman

We believe in His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Said's vision to create a Knowledge Society in Oman and want to play a part in this vision by providing professional Web/Mobile/Software development services at affordable rate. Moreover, we would like to provide free workshops to share our knowledge in various technical domains for the empowerment of Omani People.

Web and Software Development in Muscat, Sohar, Salalah

We target our Web, Mobile and Software development services to consumers in Muscat, Sohar and Salalah. Muscat, Sohar and Salalah have the highest concentration of companies that may benefit from our services.
